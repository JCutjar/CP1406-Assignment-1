Goals

The goals of the CossiePlay website are to bring in more revenue for the company by selling more custom-made costumes and encouraging more people to attend the monthly parties held by CossiePlay.

Success Evaluation

Success of the website can be determined by monitoring the number of orders placed for custom-made costumes, as well as the number of attendees at the monthly parties. To ensure this success isn’t caused by some other factor (word of mouth, etc.) there should be a short survey of party attendees with questions such as “How did you hear about our parties?”

Target Audience

The target audience for the CossiePlay website is high school kids and young adults (16-23), gender is not a factor in the target audience. The site will target people of those ages through use of pictures featuring individuals of a similar age cosplaying.
